Thera Bank - Personal Loan Campaign - Logistic Regression¶

Context:¶

This case is about a bank (Thera Bank) whose management wants to explore ways of converting its liability customers to personal loan customers (while retaining them as depositors). A campaign that the bank ran last year for liability customers showed a healthy conversion rate of over 9% success. This has encouraged the retail marketing department to devise campaigns with better target marketing to increase the success ratio with a minimal budget.

Objective:¶

The classification goal is to predict the likelihood of a liability customer buying personal loans.

Dataset:¶

 ID: Customer ID  Age: Custome's age in completed years  Experience: #years of professional experience  Income: Annual income of the customer ($000)  ZIP Code: Home Address ZIP code.  Family: Family size of the customer  CCAvg: Avg. spending on credit cards per month ($000)  Education: Education Lvel. 1: Undergrad; 2: Graduate; 3: Advanced/Professional  Mortgage: Value of house mortgage if any. ($000)  Personal Loan: Did this customer accept the personal loan offered in the last campaign?  Securities Account: Doesthe customer have a securities account with the bank?  CD Account: Doesthe customer have a certificate of deposit (CD) account with the bank?  Online: Does the customer use internet banking facilities?  Credit card: Doesthe customer use a credit card issued by the bank?

Interpretation of Pseudo R^2¶

A pseudo R^2 of 60% indicates that 60% of the uncertainty of the intercept only model is explained by the full model

Calculate the odds ratio from the coef using the formula odds ratio=exp(coef)¶

Calculate the probability from the odds ratio using the formula probability = odds / (1+odds)¶

Business Reason¶

Confusion matrix means

True Positive (observed=1,predicted=1):

Predicted that personal loan campagin will be right fit for the customer and the customer was eligibile for personal loan

False Positive (observed=0,predicted=1):

Predicted that personal compaign will be right fit for the custoer and the customer was not eligibile for personal loan

True Negative (observed=0,predicted=0):

Predicted that customeris not eligible for personal loan compaign and the customer was not eligibile for personal loan

False Negative (observed=1,predicted=0):

Predicted that customer is not eligible for personal loan campaign and the customer was eligibile for personal loan

Here the bank wants to market personal loan campaign to the people who are best fit for the personal loan i.e. less number of False Positive, if FP is high bank personal loan campagin will not be successful. So that the bank doesn't lose money on selling to the people who are not eligible for the loan. Hence Precision is the important metric. This model predicts the odds of an existing customer signing up for a personal loan campaign based on the customer attributes and relationship they have with the the bank.
